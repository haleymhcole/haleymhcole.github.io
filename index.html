Hi, I’m Haley!

I’m an artist specializing in acrylics, mixed-media, and prints. Over the years, my creative adventures have taken me to all sorts of hobbies, like candle-making or book-binding, but my root is the legacy of female artists in my family. My grandma taught my mom how to paint, and my mom then taught me — I carry that legacy with me to every brush stroke.
Follow my artistic journey, see art videos, get inspiration, and more on my Instagram: @haleycolecreates.
